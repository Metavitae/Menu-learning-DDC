🍹 Signature Cocktails (1oz pours)
Classic
🥃 Classic Margarita

Ingredients

Tequila Blanco2 oz
Fresh lime juice1 oz
Triple Sec / Cointreau1 oz
Salt rim + lime wheelgarnish

Method

1
Salt the rim: rub lime edge, dip in salt.
2
Add all liquids + ice to shaker. Shake 12–15 sec.
3
Strain into ice-filled rocks glass. Lime wheel garnish.
🧠"2-1-1 shake till done."
Frozen
🧊 Frozen Margarita

Ingredients

Tequila Blanco2 oz
Fresh lime juice1 oz
Triple Sec1 oz
Crushed ice1 cup
Fruit puree (optional)2 oz

Method

1
Add all to blender with ice.
2
Blend until smooth and slushy.
3
Pour into salt-rimmed glass.
🧠"Same as classic — just blend it."

🌿 Mezcal Negroni

Ingredients

Mezcal1 oz
Campari1 oz
Sweet Vermouth1 oz
Orange peel twistgarnish

Method

1
Combine all in mixing glass with ice.
2
STIR — do NOT shake! 30 seconds.
3
Strain over big ice. Express orange peel.
🧠Equal 1-1-1. MCV. Always STIR.
💡 Spirit-only = STIR. With juice = SHAKE.
