🍹 Signature Cocktails (1oz pours)
Signature · Classic
🥃 Classic Margarita
Mexico's most iconic — always order-worthy

Ingredients

Tequila Blanco2 oz
Fresh lime juice1 oz
Triple Sec / Cointreau1 oz
Salt (rim) + lime wheelgarnish

Method

1
Salt the rim: rub lime edge, dip in salt.
2
Add all liquids + ice to shaker. Shake 12–15 sec.
3
Strain into ice-filled rocks glass. Lime wheel garnish.
🧠 Mnemonic"2-1-1 and shake till done." Tequila double, lime & triple sec single. Traffic light on tequila: Green (lime) → Orange (triple sec) → Spirit leads.
Signature · Frozen
🧊 Frozen Margarita
Same 2-1-1 recipe — blended with ice

Ingredients

Tequila Blanco2 oz
Fresh lime juice1 oz
Triple Sec1 oz
Crushed ice1 cup
Fruit puree (strawberry/mango etc.)2 oz optional

Method

1
Add all to blender including ice (and fruit puree if flavored).
2
Blend until smooth and slushy.
3
Pour into salt-rimmed glass. Garnish with fruit.
🧠 Mnemonic"Same as classic — just blend it." Rule: Shake = rocks. Blend = frozen. Fruit puree makes it flavored.
Signature · Light
🍊 Paloma
Mexico's most popular drink — bigger than margaritas locally

Ingredients

Tequila Blanco2 oz
Fresh lime juice½ oz
Grapefruit soda (Jarritos/Squirt)top up
Salt rim + lime wedgegarnish

Method

1
Salt rim of highball glass. Fill with ice.
2
Pour tequila + lime juice over ice.
3
Top with grapefruit soda. Stir gently. Lime wedge.
🧠 MnemonicPaloma = "Dove" in Spanish. Light and airy like a dove. "Dove flies high on grapefruit sky." Highball glass, not rocks.
Signature · Smoky
🌿 Mezcal Negroni
Spirit-forward — smoky, bitter, sophisticated

Ingredients

Mezcal1 oz
Campari1 oz
Sweet Vermouth1 oz
Orange peel twistgarnish

Method

1
Combine all in mixing glass with ice.
2
STIR (do not shake!) 30 seconds.
3
Strain over big ice cube in rocks glass. Express orange peel, drop in.
🧠 MnemonicEqual parts 1-1-1. "MCV = Mexico City Victory." Always STIR — shaking clouds a spirit-only drink. Orange peel, not a slice.
💡 Key rule: Spirit-only cocktails = STIR. Cocktails with juice or cream = SHAKE.
Signature · Beer Cocktail
🍺 Michelada
Mexico's spicy beer cocktail — perfect with food

Ingredients

Mexican lager (Modelo/Corona/Pacífico)1 bottle
Fresh lime juice1 oz
Hot sauce (Valentina/Tabasco)3–5 dashes
Worcestershire sauce2 dashes
Tajín + salt rimrim

Method

1
Tajín + salt rim on tall glass or mug.
2
Add lime juice, hot sauce, Worcestershire to glass.
3
Pour beer slowly down the side. Stir gently. Lime wedge garnish.
🧠 Mnemonic"Beer got spicy and sour at the cantina." Lime (sour) + hot sauce (spicy) + beer (base). Tajín rim = the crown. Pour beer LAST.
